GENERAL
What exactly is MAT8? +

MAT8 is a proprietary algorithmic trading system that trades TQQQ (3x leveraged Nasdaq-100 ETF) using a momentum strategy with a multi-layer volatility protection system.

The algorithm runs on QuantConnect's cloud infrastructure and executes trades directly in your Interactive Brokers account — fully automated, no human discretion involved.

Is MAT8 a fund? Am I giving you my money? +

No. MAT8 is not a fund. Your money stays in your own IBKR account at all times. We never hold, touch, or custody your funds.

We operate as an authorised trading advisor on your account — we can place trades but cannot withdraw funds. You retain full control and can revoke access or withdraw at any time.

What markets and instruments does MAT8 trade? +

The primary instrument is TQQQ — the ProShares UltraPro QQQ, a 3x leveraged Nasdaq-100 ETF listed on NASDAQ.

Supporting instruments used for signal generation (not necessarily held): GLD (gold ETF), VIXY (VIX short-term futures ETF), and TLT (20+ year Treasury bond ETF).

ACCOUNT SETUP
What do I need to get started? +

You need an Interactive Brokers account with access to US equities. Individual or corporate accounts both work. The account must be funded and approved for trading leveraged ETFs.

Minimum recommended capital: USD 10,000. The algorithm is most effective at larger account sizes where IBKR commissions represent a smaller fraction of position sizes.

How do you access my IBKR account? +

You grant MAT8 access as a Third-Party Advisor via IBKR's Advisor platform. This gives trade-execution-only access — we cannot withdraw funds, change account settings, or access statements.

You can revoke this access at any time through your IBKR Client Portal in under 60 seconds.

Can I use an existing IBKR account or do I need a new one? +

Either works. If you have an existing IBKR account, we can set up a sub-account dedicated to MAT8 so your existing holdings and strategies remain completely separate.

If you don't have an IBKR account yet, opening one is straightforward — we provide a complete setup guide as part of onboarding.

RISK & RETURNS
What is the maximum drawdown I should expect? +

Based on backtesting and live performance, the maximum drawdown has been approximately 18–22%. This is significantly lower than holding TQQQ directly (which has experienced drawdowns of 80%+).

The VIXY-based protection layers are specifically designed to exit positions before major drawdown events accelerate. However, no algorithm can guarantee protection against sudden gap-down opens or flash crashes.

Are the returns audited or verified? +

Performance data on this site is pulled directly from IBKR via the Flex Query API — it represents actual account data, not backtested or hypothetical results.

The data pipeline: IBKR account → Flex Query API → GitHub Actions (weekly) → performance dashboard. No manual data entry.

What happens during a major market crash? +

The VIXY fear detection system is designed to identify elevated volatility regimes and exit positions. During events like March 2020 and late 2022, the system would have detected the VIXY spike and reduced or exited TQQQ exposure.

That said, no system is perfect — a sudden overnight gap-down (e.g. circuit breaker events) could still result in losses before the algorithm can respond at market open.

FEES & COSTS
What are the fees for using MAT8? +

MAT8 charges a monthly management fee based on account size. Specific fee schedules are provided during the onboarding consultation.

In addition, you will incur IBKR's standard trading commissions (typically USD 0.005 per share for US equities, subject to IBKR's pricing) and any exchange/regulatory fees on executed trades.

Are there margin or financing costs? +

TQQQ is purchased as a cash equity — no margin borrowing is required by the algorithm itself. The 3x leverage is embedded within the ETF structure by ProShares, not applied at the account level.

If your IBKR account uses margin for other purposes, margin interest from those activities would be separate and not related to MAT8's trading.

TECHNICAL
What happens if the internet goes down or QuantConnect has an outage? +

QuantConnect's cloud infrastructure runs with high availability. In the event of an outage, open positions remain in your IBKR account unchanged — no automatic liquidation occurs.

IBKR itself has its own risk management systems that will protect your account from extreme adverse moves even during platform outages.

How is my performance data kept up to date? +

Every Monday at 9AM SGT, a GitHub Actions workflow automatically calls the IBKR Flex Query API, fetches the latest account data, and updates the performance dashboard JSON file.

The pipeline is: IBKR Flex API → Python sync script → GitHub Actions → GitHub Pages → this dashboard. Fully automated with no manual steps.
