Overview

MOMENTUM-DRIVEN. FEAR-AWARE.

MAT8 D-Series is a systematic momentum strategy trading TQQQ — the 3x leveraged Nasdaq-100 ETF — within a dual-tranche architecture that separates entry logic from exit logic.

The algorithm runs continuously on QuantConnect's cloud infrastructure, deploying orders directly to your Interactive Brokers account via the IBKR API. No human discretion. No emotional override.

The core insight: momentum in leveraged ETFs is real, but requires a robust fear-detection layer to avoid catastrophic drawdowns during volatility spikes.

Signal Architecture
VIXY
Fear Guard
Multi-layer volatility detection. Overnight spike, 5-day structural elevation, and intraday deterioration exits.
GLD
Momentum Gate
Gold strength signal confirms risk-on environment and gates T2 tranche entry and exit rotation.
TLT
Rate Filter
Treasury rate direction filter. Blocks entries during sustained rate-rising environments hostile to growth.

State saved to ObjectStore. Performance metrics updated. Weekly reporting data written if Monday run.
