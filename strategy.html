Overview

MOMENTUM-DRIVEN.
FEAR-AWARE.

MAT8 D-Series is a fully automated systematic strategy that trades US equity and commodity instruments using momentum signals combined with a proprietary volatility regime detection layer.

The algorithm runs continuously on QuantConnect's cloud infrastructure, deploying orders directly into your Interactive Brokers account. No human discretion. No emotional override.

At its core, the strategy seeks to capture momentum in high-beta instruments while dynamically detecting fear regimes and reducing exposure before drawdowns accelerate.
